# Laptop Opened, Battery Replacement and Internal Inspection

Laptop opened to replace battery and inspect internal components after boot failure.

The back cover was removed to access internal hardware. The original battery was disconnected and removed, and a new battery was installed and connected securely.

A visual inspection was performed on key components including the motherboard, cooling system, RAM, and storage devices.

The hard drive was also removed to help isolate the issue and confirm hardware was functioning correctly.

No visible physical damage was observed during inspection.

This step was performed to replace a potentially faulty battery and determine whether the boot issue was hardware-related before proceeding with further troubleshooting.
