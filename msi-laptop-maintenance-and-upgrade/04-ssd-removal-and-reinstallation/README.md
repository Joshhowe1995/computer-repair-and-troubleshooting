# SSD Removal and Reinstallation

Removed and reinstalled the M.2 SSD to confirm proper handling and connection.

Process:
- Located SSD securing screw
- Unscrewed and removed SSD from slot
- Reinserted SSD at correct angle
- Secured SSD back into place

Outcome:
- SSD seated correctly
- Connection confirmed stable

This demonstrates correct handling of internal storage components.
